# Shrouded Keep Adventure - Steam Page Draft

## Short Description

Enter a moonlit keep above a blue fog ravine. Fight shadow figures, claim moon seals, uncover ruined lore, and climb toward the Moon-Crowned Keeper.

## Long Description

`Shrouded Keep Adventure` is a cinematic 2.5D/3D dark fantasy action-adventure set inside a ruined fortress under a violet star sky. Cross blue fog ravines, battle hostile figures, unlock moon gates, find hidden lore stones, and survive the keep's crowned threats.

The game is being built in Godot 4 with GDScript and a strict CC0/public-domain asset policy.

## Feature Bullets

- Explore six planned fortress zones built around moonlit ruins and blue fog.
- Fight readable shadow threats with sword attacks, dodges, and boss encounters.
- Collect moon seals to unlock gates and push deeper into the keep.
- Discover lore stones and secrets that reveal the fortress history.
- Checkpoint save flow designed for a 2-3 hour premium indie adventure.
