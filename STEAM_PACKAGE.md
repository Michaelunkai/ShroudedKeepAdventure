# Steam Package Plan

This folder tracks the outside-of-game materials needed for a commercial release.

## Required Store Materials

- Short description
- Long description
- Feature bullets
- Tags and genre positioning
- At least 8 screenshots from the real executable
- Gameplay trailer capture plan
- Capsule/header/library art
- Logo and executable icon
- Press-kit copy
- Credits and CC0 asset license manifest

## Current Positioning Copy

**Short description:**  
Enter a moonlit keep above a blue fog ravine. Fight shadow figures, claim moon seals, and climb through violet ruins toward the Moon-Crowned Keeper.

**Feature bullets:**

- Cinematic 2.5D/3D dark fantasy adventure
- Sword combat with dodges, telegraphed enemy attacks, and bosses
- Six planned zones across a ruined moon fortress
- Secrets, lore fragments, checkpoints, and gate progression
- Built with strict CC0/public-domain asset policy

## Art Direction

Black stone, cobalt fog, violet stars, magenta moon, ruined towers, readable silhouettes, and threatening enemy figures.
